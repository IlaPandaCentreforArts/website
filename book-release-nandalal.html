Book Release of Nandalal Bose O Santiniketan
& IPCA MFA Scholarship 2020 Award Ceremony

31st March 2021
Bhubaneswar

IPCA published a book, Nandalal Bose O Santiniketan by Shri Durgaprasad Das,an eminent senior artist of Odisha. Shri Das, an alumnus of Viswa Bharati and the former Principal of College of Art and Craft, Khallikote is known for his command of over Indian style of painting with elements of Chinese paintings embedded in them. This book is about Shri Das’s close interaction with Shri Nandalal Bose while he was a student at Kala Bhavana, Santiketan.

As part of our mandate to promote art students from Odisha, we support scholarships for graduate artists to pursue a Master’s in Fine Arts in reputed universities outside Odisha. The idea is to enable them to hone their core competencies, as well as, synergise their specialised skills with emerging trends in the contemporary art world. For the year 2020 - 2021, five such graduate students were selected for IPCA MFA Scholarship.
