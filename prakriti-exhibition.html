Prakriti: Women & Nature



8th Mar 2021

Ila Panda Centre for Arts celebrates International Women's Day 2021 through an exploration of the link between women and nature through visual art. 33 women artists take you through an emotional narrative essaying this unique bond.

Jury

"The paintings and artworks shared by women artists reveal a sophistication and curiosity about the world in which they live. Nature, appreciation of natural beauty and the powerful messages are a shared language among these works exhibited."

"Congratulations to IPCA and the participating artists for the endeavour. It was immensely pleasing to see the works of women artists that encompass their sensitivities towards their works which advances the mission. All the very best and Happy International Women's Day."
