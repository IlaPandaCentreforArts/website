PRAKRITI: Nature and Women

On March 8th 2021, we all will be celebrating International Women's Day! In honour of this day, IPCA invites Odia women artists to submit artworks by them inspired by "Prakriti"-nature and women. These artworks will be exhibited online on March 8th 2021 at IPCA website and Facebook.



CONDITIONS OF PARTICIPATION

  • Participation is open for Odia women artists (18 years of age & above) to send their Painting, Drawings, Photographs, Graphics and Sculpture or any other creative work.
  • Participants must follow the theme; i.e., "Prakriti-nature and women". Technique and work size is not a limitation.
  • Minimum of one and maximum of three works will be accepted from individual participants. The submitted artwork/s must have brief note/s on them.
  • Both national and international artists can participate in this online exhibition.
  • Participation is free.
  • All participants will be provided participation certificate by e-mail.
  • The decision of the jury in selecting artwork for online display will be final and cannot be challenged.
  • The deadline for sending in entries is 5th March 2021.
  • Participants need to fill up the form available on IPCA website. A self-photograph along with artwork/s (both in JPEG format with a resolution of 300 dpi and size not more than 5MB) to be mailed to ilapandacentreforarts@gmail.com
  • Participants must ensure with a piece declaration that they own the rights to the artwork/s submitted by them.
  • Participants may submit existing artworks/ if that adheres to the theme i.e., "Prakriti-nature and women".
